Welcome to my Master’s in Library & Information Science portfolio site!

The purpose of this website is to serve as an ePortfolio to showcase projects I have completed during the course of my MLIS. The projects highlighted on this site demonstrate my mastery of the following learning objectives of the Library and Information Science Master’s Program:
Application of the core principles, ethics, values, and body of knowledge to questions in library and information science, according to area of specialty.
Application of management principles and interdisciplinary best practices in library and information agency environments.
Competency in information organization and with understanding and evaluating information and communication technologies relevant to the information professions.
Assessment of community needs, formulation of plans to respond to users of information agencies, and instruction of users in using informational resources.
These projects were completed either for a class in my Master’s program or in the course of my work as an Academic Library Fellow at the University of Missouri - Kansas City Library.
