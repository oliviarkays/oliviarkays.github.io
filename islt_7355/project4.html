Description

I researched Green and Gold Open Access publishing pathways in order to develop infographic guides for researchers at the University of Missouri - Kansas City, and subsequently adapted these infographics into accessible LibGuides. Over the summer of 2023, I read various books and articles related to open access publishing in order to get a grounding in the area. I then began compiling tools and resources to provide to researchers. Finally, I combined the research and the tools and resources to create an infographic that walks researchers through the steps of finding and evaluating an open access journal or repository and how to submit their publications properly in order to increase the reach of their research.

Reflection

This project was one of my longest running, with the research taking place over the majority of the summer and the synthesization into infographics and LibGuides running into the fall. The images shown above are the LibGuide versions of the Green and Gold OA publishing guides, and meet accessibility standards while replicating the infographic format and maintaining visual engagement. I am hoping to revamp these following the completion of this course, in order to apply my newly acquired knowledge of CSS and HTML.
